Overview

Purpose

To be a leading company in nature tourism, recognized for its excellent quality of service. Being an example of local effort that translates into global benefits.

Audience

We invite all people aged 14 and over to participate in rafting activities. Join this unique experience in contact with nature

What is Rafting?

Are recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

It is dangerous?

By using modern rafts and strict adherence to all safety regulations, the likelihood of an injury is minimized. Rafting thus became a very safe sport.

Is it expensive?

Price start at $30 and up"
